FAQs

ARMBLOOD is a mobile app connecting blood donors with those in urgent need, using an intelligent algorithm to quickly match requirements with nearby donors, ensuring an immediate response.

ARMBLOOD enables blood recipients to create alerts with their requirements, while nearby registered donors receive notifications to connect and coordinate blood donations quickly.

ARMBLOOD connects donors and recipients globally, extending geographical boundaries to facilitate blood donations worldwide, with current availability in India and Vietnam.

Download ARMBLOOD for free from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) by searching "ARMBLOOD" and following the installation instructions.

At ARMBLOOD, we prioritize user privacy, securely storing and sharing personal information with potential donors only with your approval, ensuring strict confidentiality and data protection.

Anyone who meets blood donation criteria can register as a donor on ARMBLOOD, welcoming individuals in good health dedicated to saving lives.

ARMBLOOD allows blood recipients to review donor profiles, facilitating communication and informed decision-making based on availability, location, and preferences.

ARMBLOOD desires a quick response from nearby donors through its efficient system, considering factors like location, blood type compatibility, and availability.

ARMBLOOD is a free app for donors and recipients. We aim to make blood donation accessible and encourage life-saving connections without cost.

We value user feedback and quickly address concerns through app support or email, providing a responsive and attentive approach to user needs.
